Obudu Village

This is a small community located three hours out of lagos, on the east side. these people are predominantly farmers, it has a population of three thousand and has a skeletal community clinic which is not equiped to serve even half of the population. they are alot of children who fall sick very often and most die because of lack proper health care.

Things That Are Required

  • immunization
  • maternity drugs and care
  • polio vaccines
  • malaria treated nets
  • malaria drugs
  • food and clothing

Medical Personnels Required

  • mdical doctors
  • midwives and nurses
  • medical lab technicians
  • anyone willing to help
